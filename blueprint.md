
# Animal World Cup Game

## Overview

This project is an interactive "Animal World Cup" game where users can choose their favorite animal from a series of matchups. The game will consist of 30 rounds, and a final ranking of the animals will be displayed at the end. The entire experience will be in Korean.

## Design and Features

### Visuals
*   **Theme:** Fun, engaging, and visually appealing.
*   **Layout:** A clean, responsive, and centered layout that works on both mobile and web.
*   **Colors:** A vibrant and energetic color palette.
*   **Fonts:** Expressive and easy-to-read typography.
*   **Images:** High-quality images of animals and dinosaurs.
*   **Effects:** Subtle animations and hover effects to make the interface more interactive.

### Gameplay
*   **Matchups:** In each round, two animals are presented to the user.
*   **Selection:** The user clicks on the image of the animal they prefer.
*   **Rounds:** The game will have a total of 30 matchups.
*   **Ranking:** After all rounds are completed, a final ranking of the animals will be displayed, showing which animals were chosen the most.
